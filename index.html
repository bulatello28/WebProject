About us

Wrestling Mania is one of the best wrestling schools not only in the Russian Federation, but all over the world. We teach wrestling of a variety of styles, be it: Sambo, Greco-Roman, Belt wrestling or freestyle. We train athletes of various age categories: boys and girls 10-15 years old, adults 16-30 years old.

Treining Points

Our training facilities are equipped with the latest attributes. In addition to the main training hall for wrestling, we also have swimming pools and gyms where our athletes can strengthen their physical and spiritual health by going through grueling workouts.

The 3 most popular types of wrestling of our school

Belt wrestling is an ancient type of wrestling, consisting in a single combat of two belted wrestlers in a stand. Athletes are in a fight all the time, holding each other by the belt, and each of the rivals is trying to knock the other down and win.

Greco-Roman wrestling is a European type of wrestling in which an athlete, through a certain arsenal of technical actions (techniques), tries to unbalance an opponent and press his shoulder blades to the carpet.
